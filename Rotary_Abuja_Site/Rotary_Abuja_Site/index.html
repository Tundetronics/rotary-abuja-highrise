The Pulse of District 9127

Abuja High Rise stands as a leadership hub within the newly formed District 9127. Our reach extends beyond the capital, addressing humanitarian needs in 16 Northern states including Kaduna, Kano, Borno, and Sokoto.

We are dedicated to the 7 Areas of Focus, ensuring sustainable impact in maternal health, basic education, and peace-building.
